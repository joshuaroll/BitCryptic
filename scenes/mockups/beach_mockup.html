Anagram Beach: Scene Mockups

Step 0: Arrival at Anagram Beach

Warm sand shifts beneath your feet as you step onto Anagram Beach. Shells arranged in clusters spell out scrambled words, and messages in bottles wash up with every wave, each one a jumbled clue.

Step 1: The Bottle's Message

A bottle rolls to your feet with a note inside: 'The cove hides beneath the cliffs. Seek where letters meet the stone.' Following the cliffside, you find a rock face carved with faded symbols and a narrow vine-covered passage.

Step 2: The Stone Door

The passage entrance is blocked by a heavy stone door. Two handles are carved into it, labeled 'push' and 'pull.' But scrawled in chalk beneath them: 'TIFL OT NEPO.' The letters seem scrambled...

Step 3: The Passage Opens

You grip the bottom edge and heave upward. The stone door slides up into the cliff with a satisfying rumble, revealing a hidden cove beyond. Phosphorescent writing shimmers across the walls. Cluey Cove awaits.
