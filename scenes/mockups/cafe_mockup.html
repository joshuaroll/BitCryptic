Cryptic Cafe — Scene Mockups

Scene 0 — Steps 0 & 1
Narrator

"You push open the creaky door of the Cryptic Cafe. Warm lamplight spills across mismatched wooden tables. The walls are covered in framed puzzles, some solved, most not, left behind by Cryptic Voyagers past."

Barista

"Welcome, newbie!" A barista with ink-stained fingers and reading glasses perched on her nose slides a steaming cup toward you. "Take a seat, warm up. That cup's got a little puzzle on it, if you're curious."

Scene 2 — Step 2
Narrator

Written on the cup in looping script is a cryptic clue: "Something instantly perky, starts a mouthful (3)."

Barista

"That's an initial letters clue. 'Starts' tells you to take the first letter of each word before it. Three letters, and the definition is 'a mouthful.'"

Scene 3 — Steps 3 & 4
Barista

"Sip! Exactly right." She smiles and reaches for a lever behind the espresso machine. A section of the wall slides open, revealing a cobblestone path. "That leads to Town Square. The heart of the whole island. You've earned it."

Narrator

Through the hidden passage, you glimpse a bustling square with a grand fountain at its center. The barista gives you a wave. "Come back for a refill anytime."
