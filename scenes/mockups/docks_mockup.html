Decoder Docks — Scene Mockups

Step 0 — docks_0

You wake to the sound of waves lapping against wooden planks. Salt stings your eyes as you blink into consciousness. A tattered red boat rocks gently beside a weathered dock, its rope fraying in the breeze.

Step 1 — docks_1

A lantern flickers at the end of the pier. Pinned to its post is a soggy note, the ink barely legible: "WELcome TO BIT CRYPTIC WORLD. NOTHinG IS AS IT SEEMS." At the bottom, in smaller writing: ".S.P .EMOH EMOCLEW"

Steps 2, 3, 5 — docks_2 (shared)
Dock Keeper

A weathered dock keeper steps out from a nearby shack, lantern in hand. His face is lined by years of salt and sun, but his eyes are warm. "Easy now, easy. You took quite a tumble out there." He wraps a dry blanket around your shoulders. "You're safe. This is Bit Cryptic World."

Step 3 — docks_3
Dock Keeper

"See that note on the post? This island loves its puzzles. Even the welcome letter has a trick to it." He taps the bottom of the soggy note. "That line at the end. .S.P is just P.S. written backwards. And if .S.P is reversed, then the rest of that line is too. Give it a read the right way round."

Step 4 — docks_4 (puzzle)
Dock Keeper

He holds the lantern closer so you can read the reversed postscript: ".S.P .EMOH EMOCLEW" The .S.P tells you it's all written backwards. What does the message say when you reverse it?

Step 5 — docks_5
Dock Keeper

"Welcome home." He grins and the lantern seems to burn a little brighter. "That's what this island says to everyone who arrives. You belong here, friend." He nods toward the dark treeline. "When you're ready, the forest path will take you deeper. The trees have their own puzzles, but they're good company once you learn to listen."

Step 6 — docks_6 (complete)

A path through the trees shimmers into view beyond the docks. The dock keeper presses a worn compass into your palm, etched with strange symbols. "For the road. Come back anytime you need a rest." He tips his hat and heads back to his shack, whistling.
