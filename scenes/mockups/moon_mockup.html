The Mark on The Moon

Scene 0: The Lunar Surface

The moon's surface is silent and still. Craters stretch in every direction under a sky full of stars. In the distance, a small dome glows with warm light.

Scene 1: Inside Mark's Dome
Mark

"Ah, a visitor. I don't get many of those." He looks up from a half-finished crossword, pen in hand. "I'm Mark. I write clues up here. The silence helps me think."

Scene 2: Mark's First Clue
Mark

"Most solvers can't handle my clues. They're... particular. But you made it all the way here, so perhaps you're different. Let me test you."

"Blemish is also a target (4)"

Scene 3: Mark's Second Clue
Mark

"Yes, that's my name and my nature. I leave my mark on every puzzle." He almost smiles. "Here's a real challenge."

"Lunar feature from confused tracer (6)"

Scene 4: Mark's Third Clue
Mark

"Impressive. Not many get that one." He leans back in his chair. "One more. This is the kind of clue I'm most proud of."

"Cow's sound before November reveals orbiting body (4)"

Scene 5: Mark's Farewell
Mark

"Three for three. You solved a double definition, an anagram, and a charade. Not bad for a tourist." He returns to his crossword. "You're welcome here anytime. I'll have new clues waiting. The moon remembers its visitors."

The Mark on The Moon is yours to visit. Whenever you return, Mark will be here, pen in hand, lost in his puzzles. The dome glows softly in the endless lunar night.
