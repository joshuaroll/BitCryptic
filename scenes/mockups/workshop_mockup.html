Puzzle Workshop

Step 0: Workshop Interior

The Puzzle Workshop hums with life. Crossword grids line the shelves, puzzle boxes gleam in brass lamplight, and gears turn slowly in the warm gloom.

Step 1: Cryptic Croc at Workbench
Cryptic Croc

"Welcome to the Puzzle Workshop! I've been tinkering with something special for you." A crocodile wearing a golden crown and scarf stands behind the workbench, one clawed hand resting near a brass contraption.

Step 2: Anagram Engine (Puzzle)
Cryptic Croc

"This is the Anagram Engine! The letter wheels are spinning. Rearrange them to spell the answer. Here's your clue:"

"Ache badly after beginning of boating, on the shore" (5)

Step 3: Machine Solved, Coordinates Slide Out
Cryptic Croc

"SNAP-tastic! You cracked it!" The machine clicks into motion. Gears whir faster, sparks fly upward, and a brass plate slides out bearing coordinates pointing south.

Step 4: Golden Beam to Coastline
Cryptic Croc

"See that beam? It's pointing toward the coast. Your next adventure awaits!" The Anagram Engine projects a golden beam southward, particles of light streaming through the workshop opening toward the distant coastline.
