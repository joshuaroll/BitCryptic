Liam's Lair

Step 0: The Descent

The ground shakes as you descend into the passage beneath the cliffs. The air grows hot. Red light pulses from somewhere deep below, and the rock walls are scarred with claw marks the size of your arm.

Step 1: The Dragon's Chamber
The Dragon

"You found my lair. Impressive. But can you solve what I guard?" The dragon's voice rumbles through the cavern. "I am the keeper of the hardest clues on this island. Three you must solve to prove your worth."

Step 2: The First Clue
The Dragon

"First clue. Listen carefully." The dragon unfurls a scorched scroll.

"Treasure hidden in each estimate (5)"

Step 3: The Second Clue
The Dragon

"Correct." Smoke curls from the dragon's nostrils. "A hidden word clue. Simple enough. But the next requires a different kind of thinking." The dragon scratches the second clue into the stone floor with one enormous claw.

"This beast makes proceedings drag on (6)"

Step 4: The Final Clue
The Dragon

"Two down." The dragon rises to its full height, wings scraping the cavern ceiling. "A double definition. You have sharp eyes. But the final clue is my finest. It requires you to rearrange." The dragon breathes a thin line of fire across the wall, searing the final clue into the rock.

"Fun race, oddly, leads to the source of heat (7)"

Step 5: The Hoard is Yours
The Dragon

"Three for three. I have not seen that in a long time." The cavern glows with a warm light. "The hoard is yours to study. Return whenever you wish. I will have new clues waiting."

Liam's Lair glows around you. The scrolls and grids contain some of the most elegant cryptic clues ever constructed. This place is yours now.
