Cryptic Croc: Professor Mode

Cryptic Croc
Time for a lesson! Every cryptic clue is really two clues in one. There's always a straight definition hiding at the beginning or end, plus wordplay that builds the answer a different way. Find both, and you've cracked it!
Croc's Tip: Watch for "indicator words" — they tell you what type of wordplay is happening. Words like broken, crazy, wild signal an anagram. Words like back, returning, up signal a reversal!
