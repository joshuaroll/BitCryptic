## What is Cryptic Croc?

The mascot for **Bit Cryptic World** — a cryptic crossword puzzle game with a dark atmospheric theme (dark navy backgrounds, gold #ffd700 accents, Fredoka One + Nunito fonts). Cryptic Croc is a cute, friendly crocodile who guides players through cryptic crossword puzzles.

---

## Design Journey (in order)

### Round 1 — 5 Emotion Poses
Initial character in 5 poses: idle, thinking, teaching, celebrating, encouraging.  
Files: `cryptic_croc_*.html`  
**Verdict:** Good animation concepts, used as pose reference going forward.

### Round 2 — 5 Body Shape Concepts
Explored wildly different silhouettes: round, lanky, chibi, stocky, sleek.  
Files: `concept_1_round.html` through `concept_5_sleek.html`  
**Verdict:** Round body chosen as the winner.

### Round 3 — 5 "More Crocodile" Variants of Round
Made the round body more distinctly crocodilian and less monkey-like.  
Files: `round_v1_snappy.html` through `round_v5_swampy.html`  
Key changes: wide-set eyes on raised bumps, slit pupils, flat snout with nostrils at tip, scaly texture, back scutes, clawed toes, belly banding.  
**Verdict:** V1 (Snappy) chosen as the base going forward.

### Round 4 — Refinements to V1
- Removed all sharp teeth (user doesn't want teeth)
- Replaced checkerboard belly with natural horizontal belly banding
- Upgraded tail to thick solid shape with serrated dorsal ridges
- Fixed tail attachment to body

### Round 5 — 5 Smile Variants (crooked, not rounded)
Explored asymmetric/crooked smiles: lopsided, zigzag, crooked corner, S-curve, sly side grin.  
Files: `smile_v1_lopsided.html` through `smile_v5_sly.html`  
**Verdict:** Zigzag (V2) chosen for further iteration.

### Round 6 — 5 W-Smile Variants
Wide W-shaped mouth like a real croc grin: classic, wide, deep, asymmetric, soft.  
Files: `wsmile_v1_classic.html` through `wsmile_v5_soft.html`  
**Verdict:** Moved to wider proportions.

### Round 7 — 5 Wide Kawaii Variants
Rebuilt with much wider/squatter proportions inspired by kawaii reference. Small centred W-mouth, big cheek blushes, wider head and body.  
Files: `wide_v1.html` through `wide_v5.html`  
**Verdict:** Angular W (V5) smile chosen, but user preferred the ROUND BODY from previous edit over the wide body.

### Round 8 — Final Base Locked
Combined angular W smile (from wide_v5) with the round body (from wsmile series). Added cheek blushes.  
**File: `wsmile_v1_classic.html`** ← THIS IS THE CURRENT BASE TEMPLATE  
**Verdict:** Base locked. User happy with this template.

## Current Base Character Traits

- **Body:** Round (rx=72, ry=82), green gradient (#4db06a → #2a7848)
- **Head:** Wide ellipse (rx=56, ry=42), flatter on top
- **Eyes:** Wide-set on raised bumps, slit pupils (#5a7a20 iris, #1a2a0a pupil), blink animation
- **Snout:** Wide flat croc snout, nostrils at tip, bumpy texture
- **Mouth:** Angular W-smile (straight-line zigzag), NO teeth, bumpy lip ridge
- **Cheeks:** Soft pink blush ellipses (opacity 0.12)
- **Monocle:** Gold circle on right eye with chain
- **Tail:** Solid shape, wide base flush to body, serrated dorsal scutes, lateral keels
- **Feet:** Splayed toes with tiny claws
- **Belly:** Lighter gradient with horizontal banding lines
- **Back:** Scale texture pattern + 3 oval scute plates
- **Animations:** breathe, blink, tailWag, jawIdle, wave (right arm)

## User Preferences (DO NOT change these)

- **No sharp teeth** — user explicitly rejected teeth in all forms
- **No checkerboard belly** — replaced with natural croc belly banding
- **Crocodile, not monkey** — wide-set eyes, flat snout, scaly texture, slit pupils
- **Crooked smile, not rounded** — angular W shape, not symmetric arcs
- **Cheek blushes** — subtle pink, kawaii influence
